## The Problem Statement

Athletes experiencing career-altering injuries face multiple crises simultaneously:

* Loss of identity built around sport
* High rates of depression, anxiety, and emotional trauma
* Sudden financial instability
* Social disconnection from teammates and community
* Chronic pain and physical uncertainty
* Lack of structured career transition pathways

Existing solutions treat these problems separately. There is no unified system addressing the full spectrum of challenges injured athletes face.
